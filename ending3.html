NATIONAL GUARD DEPLOYED



Emboldened by a lackluster public and institutional response, the federal government has, citing Title 10 of the U.S. Code, deployed the New York National Guard into the city. An incredibly controversial move, legal debates are already firing up, questioning the legitimacy of the mobilization. Nevertheless, armed soldiers pose a significant obstacle and threat to demonstrators.
